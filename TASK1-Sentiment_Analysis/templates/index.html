Purpose of the Website

A sentiment analysis website is a tool that uses natural language processing and machine learning techniques to automatically identify and extract subjective information from a given text. The purpose of a sentiment analysis website is to provide users with an understanding of the emotional tone or attitude expressed in a piece of text.

Applications of the Website

  1. Brand monitoring: track how customers feel about a brand, product or service.
  2. Market research: understand consumer sentiment about a particular topic or industry.
  3. Social media monitoring: track how users feel about a particular event, person or organization.
  4. Sentiment-based news filtering: identify positive or negative news stories.
  5. Opinion mining: extract subjective information from a large number of documents.
  6. Voice of customer analysis: understand customer feedback.
  7. Review analysis: understand customer feedback about products and services.
